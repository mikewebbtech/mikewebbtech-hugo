My shell is the glue of my CLI workflow. This series is a deep-dive into my personal build of a modular, fast, and reliable Zsh environment. I'm documenting how I moved from a cluttered "junk drawer" config to a tidy and flexible way to manage my shell interface to the tools that I use. Incorporating add-ons and enhancments like keyboard motions, contextual prompts and quality of life improvments along the way.
